About Grandma:
Bethany Mae Johnson began baking pies for her family at the young age of 16, and has been making delicious, mouth-watering pies for her family and friends ever since. She was famous in her small hometown of Roxbury, CT for her contributions to bake sales and her entries in the county fairs. At the age of 54, she opened her own small business, which is still running today. The current owners of Granny's Pies strive to carry on the greatness of Bethany Johnson's work, and have not altered any of the original recipes she provided for the company.
